Privacy Policy

Resumatic — AI Resume Builder

Last updated: June 10, 2025

📋 Introduction

Resumatic ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered resume builder for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data you enter into your resume stays in your control — we only process it to provide the service.

📦 Information We Collect

Category What we collect Why
Account Name, email address, profile photo URL Sign-in & personalization
Resume content Text you enter (work history, skills, education, etc.) Core app functionality & AI suggestions
Subscription Plan type (free / Pro), purchase token, expiry date Billing & feature access
Usage data AI request counts, feature usage frequency Rate-limiting, abuse prevention
Device info Android version, device model (via crash reporting) Debugging & stability

We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts, microphone audio, or camera images.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Generate AI-powered resume content suggestions using your resume text
  • Verify purchases and manage Pro subscription access
  • Store and sync your resumes across devices
  • Enforce rate limits to prevent abuse of AI features
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app stability

🤖 AI Services & Third Parties

When you use AI features, selected resume text is sent to a large language model provider to generate suggestions. We share only the minimum text needed to fulfill the request. No request is permanently stored by us beyond your own account data.

Third Party Purpose Privacy Policy
Supabase Authentication, database, serverless functions supabase.com/privacy
Google Play Billing In-app purchases & subscription management policies.google.com/privacy
AI Provider (LLM) Resume content generation & AI suggestions Depends on configured provider (e.g. Anthropic / OpenAI)

💾 Data Storage & Retention

  • On-device: Account session details and subscription status are stored in encrypted SharedPreferences.
  • Cloud (Supabase): Your account profile and resume data are stored in our Supabase project hosted in the EU.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time (see "Your Rights" below).
  • Subscription purchase tokens are retained for the duration of the subscription to validate access.

🔒 Security

We use industry-standard measures including HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit, Supabase Row-Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data, and server-side purchase verification via Google Play Developer API to prevent fraudulent Pro access. No security measure is perfect — please use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication on your sign-in provider.

👶 Children's Privacy

Resumatic is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

✅ Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your resume data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential data processing
