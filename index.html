Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2025

In plain English: We do not sell your data. Resume content is used only to generate AI suggestions on your behalf. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

Introduction

Resumatic is an AI-powered resume builder for Android. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the app you agree to these practices.

What We Collect

CategoryData
AccountName, email, profile photo URL
ResumeText you enter — work history, skills, education
BillingPlan type, purchase token, expiry date
UsageAI request counts (rate-limiting only)
DeviceAndroid version, model (crash reporting)

We do not collect GPS location, contacts, microphone, or camera data.

AI & Third Parties

When you use AI features, your resume text is sent to an LLM provider to generate suggestions. We share only the minimum text needed.

ServicePurpose
SupabaseAuth, database, functions
Google PlayIn-app purchases
Anthropic / OpenAIAI resume suggestions

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Supabase Row-Level Security ensures you can only access your own data. Purchase tokens are verified server-side via Google Play Developer API.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your resume data
  • Opt out of non-essential processing

Email Vottery.GWS@gmail.com — we respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Resumatic is not directed at children under 13 (under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us immediately if you believe a child has registered.

Retention & Storage

  • On-device: session & subscription in SharedPreferences
  • Cloud: resumes & profile in Supabase (EU region)
  • Retained while your account is active
  • Purchase tokens kept for subscription duration
